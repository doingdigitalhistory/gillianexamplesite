About This Site

Our Mission

This site serves as a demonstration of how digital tools can enhance historical research and make history more accessible to everyone. We believe in the power of technology to preserve and share our collective past.

About the Creator

Gillian is a digital history enthusiast dedicated to exploring new ways of understanding and presenting historical information. This example site showcases basic web development techniques applied to historical content.

Project Goals

  • Demonstrate best practices in digital history presentation
  • Create an accessible and user-friendly experience
  • Inspire others to explore digital history methods
  • Provide a foundation for future digital history projects
